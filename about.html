Instructions

Welcome to Guess The Number! In this game, you are provided with 50 squares from 0 to 49 and you have to guess which one out of those 50 squares is the special number! Click on the number you want to guess! If your guess is incorrect, the message "higher" or "lower" will appear to aid you in finding the right number! Try to find the number in less than 5 turns for a prize!
